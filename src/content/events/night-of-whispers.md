## What Happened

On a night when the moon was swallowed by clouds, creatures of living shadow poured from the Whispering Caves and attacked settlements across the Verdant Vale. The small temple where Kira Sunblade was being raised was among the targets.

The temple guardians fought bravely but were overwhelmed. In a moment of desperation, young Kira reached out to the divine and channeled a burst of radiant energy that drove the shadows back. It was the first recorded manifestation of divine power in the region in over a century.

## Aftermath

The attack was eventually repelled, but it left scars on the land and its people. The village council increased patrols, and the Adventurer's Guild began actively recruiting. Most importantly, it set Kira on the path to becoming a paladin.

The source of the shadow creatures was never determined — until recently.
