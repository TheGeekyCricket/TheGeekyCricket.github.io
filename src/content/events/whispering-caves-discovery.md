## What Happened

Following the clue of the dark crystal found in the Goblin Warren, the party ventured into the Whispering Caves. Deep within, they discovered an ancient magical seal carved into the cavern floor — and it was cracking.

The seal bore symbols from the Shadowblight Wars, an era most thought was purely legend. As they investigated, they were contacted by **Seraphina Duskmantle** of The Arcaneum, who had been monitoring the seal remotely from the Crystalpeak Citadel.

Seraphina revealed that the seal was one of several placed across the world to contain an ancient evil. If they all failed, the Shadowblight could return.

## Aftermath

This discovery changed the scope of the party's adventures from local heroics to a world-spanning quest. They now must travel to other seal locations to reinforce them — or find another way to stop the Shadowblight's return.

The party received an invitation to visit the Crystalpeak Citadel to learn more from Seraphina and the Arcaneum's archives.
