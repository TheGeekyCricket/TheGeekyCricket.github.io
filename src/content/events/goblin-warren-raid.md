## What Happened

When goblins began stealing grain from Thornfield's stores, the trail led to a network of tunnels beneath the abandoned old mill on the village outskirts. Barley Copperkettle posted the quest at the Adventurer's Guild, and three young heroes answered the call.

**Kira Sunblade**, **Fen Quickfoot**, and **Bramble Ironroot** ventured into the warren together for the first time. Despite some early mishaps (Fen triggered a trap, Bramble accidentally befriended a goblin's pet rat), they worked together to clear the tunnels and recover the stolen supplies.

## Significance

This was the first quest completed by the party and the beginning of their friendship. The goblin chieftain carried a strange dark crystal that Bramble sensed was connected to the same corruption affecting the forest — a thread that would become much more important later.
