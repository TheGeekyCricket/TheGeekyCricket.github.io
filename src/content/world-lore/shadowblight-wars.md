## Overview

The Shadowblight Wars were a devastating conflict that raged across the world over a thousand years ago. An entity known as **the Umbral Sovereign** — a being of pure shadow and malice — rose from the depths of the Abyss and sought to consume all light and life.

## The War

### The First Shadow

The Shadowblight began as a corruption — plants withered, animals became aggressive, and people fell into despair. At first, no one connected the scattered incidents. By the time the Umbral Sovereign revealed itself, entire regions had already fallen.

### The Alliance of Light

For the first time in history, all races united against a common threat. Humans, elves, dwarves, halflings, gnomes, tieflings, dragonborn, and more set aside centuries of conflict to form the **Alliance of Light**.

### The Sealing

Unable to destroy the Umbral Sovereign outright, the greatest mages and clerics of the Alliance devised a plan to seal it away. They created seven **Wardstones** — magical seals placed at key points across the world — that would contain the Sovereign's essence.

The ritual cost many lives, but it succeeded. The Umbral Sovereign was imprisoned, and the Shadowblight receded.

## Legacy

The Shadowblight Wars led directly to the founding of several modern institutions:

- **The Council of the Concordance** — the peace-keeping body at Crystalpeak Citadel
- **The Arcaneum** — the scholarly order that monitors the Wardstones
- **The Adventurer's Guilds** — originally militia organizations that evolved into the quest-giving bodies we know today

## The Current Threat

The Wardstones are weakening. After a millennium, the seals are beginning to crack. The corruption spreading from the Whispering Caves is just the beginning. If all seven Wardstones fail, the Umbral Sovereign will rise again — and this time, there may not be enough power in the world to stop it.
