## Description

Barley Copperkettle runs the Rusty Flagon Tavern in Thornfield Village. A portly halfling with a magnificent mustache and an even more magnificent collection of stories, Barley retired from adventuring after "one too many close calls with dragons" — though the details change every time he tells the tale.

## Role in the Story

Barley serves as the party's primary quest giver and source of local information. He has connections throughout the region from his adventuring days and often knows more than he lets on.

## Notable Quotes

> "Another round? Of course! But first, have I ever told you about the time I arm-wrestled an ogre? No? Well, pull up a chair..."

> "Now listen here, young heroes. The Whispering Caves aren't called that because of the pleasant breeze. You be careful in there."
