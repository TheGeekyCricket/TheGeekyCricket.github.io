## Description

Seraphina is a tiefling scholar who resides in the Crystal Observatory of the Crystalpeak Citadel. With silver-white hair and eyes that seem to shift color depending on the light, she is both captivating and unnerving. She speaks in riddles more often than straight answers.

## Role in the Story

Seraphina holds knowledge about the ancient corruption spreading through the land. She has been studying the connection between the Whispering Caves and an ancient evil sealed away during the Shadowblight Wars. She may become an ally — or something more complicated.

## The Arcaneum

Seraphina belongs to a secretive organization of scholars and mages called **The Arcaneum**. Their goal is to preserve dangerous knowledge so that it can never be used for evil — but not everyone agrees with their methods of hoarding secrets.
