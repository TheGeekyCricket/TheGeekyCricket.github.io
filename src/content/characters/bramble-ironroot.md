## Backstory

Bramble wandered out of the deep woods one day and simply never left Thornfield Village. The gentle firbolg speaks to plants and animals as easily as most people chat with neighbors. Bramble was sent by the Elder Grove to investigate a growing corruption in the natural world.

## Personality

Soft-spoken and kind, Bramble is often the voice of reason. Has a tendency to talk to trees mid-conversation and apologize to rocks when stepping on them. Despite the gentle nature, Bramble becomes a force of nature when friends are threatened.

## Key Moments

- **The Withering**: Discovered that plants near the Whispering Caves were dying from an unnatural blight
- **Wild Shape Awakening**: First transformed into a bear during a wolf attack — surprised everyone, including Bramble
- **The Garden**: Created a beautiful healing garden in Thornfield that provides herbs to the village healer
