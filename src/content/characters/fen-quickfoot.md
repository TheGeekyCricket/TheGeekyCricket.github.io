## Backstory

Fen grew up on the streets of a larger city before ending up in Thornfield Village after "a small misunderstanding involving a nobleman's prized chicken." Quick-witted and quicker with a blade, Fen has a heart of gold beneath all the sarcasm.

## Personality

Fen is the comic relief of the group, always cracking jokes even in dire situations. But beneath the humor lies a sharp mind and fierce loyalty to friends. Fen has a complicated relationship with authority figures.

## Key Moments

- **The Chicken Incident**: The reason Fen arrived in Thornfield (still won't give full details)
- **The Shadow Heist**: Infiltrated a bandit camp solo to recover stolen village supplies
- **Best Friends**: Formed an unlikely bond with Kira after she healed Fen's injuries from a bad fall
