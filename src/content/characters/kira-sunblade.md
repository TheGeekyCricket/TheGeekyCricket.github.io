## Backstory

Kira was raised in a small temple on the outskirts of Thornfield Village. As a child, she discovered she could channel divine energy to heal wounded animals in the forest. When the temple was attacked by shadow creatures during the Night of Whispers, she swore an oath to protect the innocent and root out darkness wherever it hides.

## Personality

Brave and compassionate, Kira leads her party with a steady hand and a warm heart. She has a habit of adopting stray animals and always gives people the benefit of the doubt — sometimes to her detriment.

## Key Moments

- **The Night of Whispers**: Defended the temple against shadow creatures at age 12
- **First Quest**: Cleared the Goblin Warren beneath Thornfield's old mill
- **Oath of Devotion**: Formally sworn before the Grand Thornwood during the Midsummer Festival
