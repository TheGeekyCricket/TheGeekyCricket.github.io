## History

Thornfield Village was founded over three hundred years ago by a band of weary travelers seeking refuge from the Shadowblight Wars. The village elder, Myranda Thornfield, planted the first seeds of what would become the Great Thornwood ??? a massive oak tree that still stands in the village center today.

## Notable Features

### The Great Thornwood
A massive oak tree at the center of the village. Its roots are said to extend deep into the earth, connecting to ley lines of ancient magic. Locals gather here for festivals and town meetings.

### The Rusty Flagon Tavern
The social hub of Thornfield. Run by **Barley Copperkettle**, a retired halfling adventurer who loves telling tales almost as much as he loves brewing ale.

### The Adventurer's Guild Hall
A modest stone building where young heroes receive their first quests. The notice board outside is always full of pleas from nearby settlements.

## Current Events

The village has recently been troubled by strange noises coming from the Whispering Caves to the north. Several livestock have gone missing, and the village council has put out a call for brave adventurers to investigate.
