## History

The Crystalpeak Citadel was carved from living crystal and stone by the Deepforge Dwarves over a thousand years ago. During the Age of Fracturing, when the races of the world were at war, the citadel served as the site of the Grand Concordance ??? the peace treaty that ended centuries of conflict.

## Notable Features

### The Hall of Echoes
A vast chamber where every word spoken reverberates endlessly. Diplomats use it for negotiations, as it's said that lies spoken here turn to silence.

### The Crystal Observatory
At the citadel's highest point, enchanted crystals allow observers to see across vast distances. Some say skilled users can even peer into other planes of existence.

### The Deepforge
The original dwarven forge still burns with elemental fire. Master smiths travel from across the world to craft legendary weapons here.

## Current Status

The citadel is currently home to the **Council of the Concordance**, representatives from each major faction who meet quarterly to discuss matters affecting the entire world.
